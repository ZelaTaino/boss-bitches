About Us

We believe in delivering curated community based on the likelihood that you and the group will click. We’re using tech to power the magic of first meeting someone, and feeling like you just get each other.

Meaningful connections should be accessible, and interesting. The options to meet and connect with people are expensive social clubs, awkward swiping apps, or random meetups. We’re here to change that.
